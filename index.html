🌾 About AgriVision Karnataka

AgriVision is a digital agritech platform designed to connect farmers, buyers, and logistics into a single ecosystem.

It enables farmers to list crops, buyers to place bids, and provides a transparent system for price comparison and decision making.

The platform aims to reduce middlemen dependency and improve farmer profitability using structured data and smart logic.
